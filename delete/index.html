Delete your Noora account & data

Halaman ini menjelaskan cara menghapus akun Anda pada aplikasi Noora — Cahaya Muslim (dikembangkan oleh Noora Deen Technology) beserta data yang terkait.

Cara meminta penghapusan

Kirim email dari alamat yang Anda gunakan untuk mendaftar ke:

support@nooradeen.com

Gunakan subjek "Permintaan Hapus Akun". Permintaan akan kami proses dalam waktu maksimal 30 hari.

Data yang dihapus

  • Akun Anda (nama dan alamat email).
  • Progres ibadah yang tersimpan di cloud (streak, ceklis shalat, hitungan dzikir, penanda bacaan, medali).

Data yang disimpan

Tidak ada data yang kami simpan setelah penghapusan, kecuali yang diwajibkan oleh hukum. Data yang hanya tersimpan di perangkat Anda akan hilang saat Anda logout atau meng-uninstall aplikasi.

This page explains how to delete your account on the Noora — Cahaya Muslim app (developed by Noora Deen Technology) and its associated data.

How to request deletion

Send an email from the address you used to register to:

support@nooradeen.com

Use the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.

Data that is deleted

  • Your account (name and email address).
  • Worship progress stored in the cloud (streaks, prayer check-ins, dhikr counts, bookmarks, medals).

Data that is retained

We retain no data after deletion, except where required by law. Data stored only on your device is removed when you log out or uninstall the app.
